## Overview

A hybrid board game that connects physical pieces and player actions to digital responses. The project explored tangible interaction through sensors, embedded electronics, and custom-built game components.

## What I worked on

- Helped develop the board’s interactive system using Arduino
- Integrated sensors, LEDs, and servo-based feedback
- Designed and modeled physical pieces in Blender
- Supported the connection between hardware, gameplay, and digital output

## Why it stands out

Mutate blends physical and digital interaction in a way that feels playful, experimental, and hands-on.
