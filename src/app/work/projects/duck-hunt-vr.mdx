## Overview

A VR reimagining of Duck Hunt built in Unity. The project focused on making the classic gameplay feel more physical and immersive through motion controls, responsive feedback, and real-time interaction.

## What I worked on

- Built shooting mechanics using VR motion controllers
- Created dynamic duck movement and hit feedback
- Added particle effects and spatial audio
- Improved interaction feel and overall performance

## Why it stands out

This project pushed the original game into a full 3D interactive space and helped me develop stronger skills in immersive design, gameplay feel, and real-time systems.
