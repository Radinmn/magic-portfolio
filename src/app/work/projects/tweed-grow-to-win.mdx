## Overview

A browser-based promotional game created with Tweed and Bottlecap Media. The project focused on keeping users engaged through interactive tasks, repeatable play sessions, and real-time score systems.

## What I worked on

- Built gameplay systems in Unity using C#
- Helped develop interactive task-based mechanics
- Supported score tracking and responsive game flow
- Contributed to a polished web-based play experience

## Why it stands out

It combines branded promotional design with gameplay systems that feel fast, interactive, and replayable.
